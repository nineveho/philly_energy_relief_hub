Energy Audits

Whether you rent or own, there are always opportunities to make your home more energy efficient, but it can be hard to know where to start. Conducting a home energy audit will help you figure out what makes the most sense for your home to decrease your energy consumption, easing the burden on your budget and on the environment. Access local experts available or conduct a home assessment independently to reveal the particular to help you discover energy-saving potential your home has.

PECO Energy Checkup

If you're a PECO customer, you can receive in-person, virtual, or phone visits from a PECO Energy Advisor to figure out how to save energy and money while making your home more comfortable.

ECA Energy Audit

Income-eligible households can discover energy-savings in their home for free with the help of experts from the Energy Coordinating Agency.

Self-Guided Energy Assessment

Take on the task of evaluating your home's energy potential yourself! A guide to support you through learning more about your home on your own time.
